DAO Governance:

$TIME is a governance token that allows holders to participate in Governance decisions of the platform, using a DAO structure. They can exercise voting rights on key elements such as Foundation grant attributions to content and creators and feature prioritization on the platform Roadmap. $TIME owners can vote themselves or delegate voting rights to other owners of their choice.

There is no single point of authority.

The money collected can not be spent unless agreed upon stakeholders.

There are special rights for token holders including Voting, Feedback & Goal Settings for future development.

Developing future watch related projects.


Staking

$TIME allows for staking, which allows for passive revenues on ZONE: you get more $TIME by staking it. This is also the only way to get valuable GEARS, needed for $WATCH creation.

Fee Capture model

5% of all transaction volume carried out in $TIME tokens (Transaction Fees) shall be allocated with 50% to the Staking Pool as rewards for token holders that Stake $TIME tokens and 50% to the ‘’Foundation.’’ A total of 26.5% of all SAND transactions goes to the Foundation.

Foundation

The role of the Foundation is to support the ecosystem of The TIME ZONE, offering grants to incentivize high-quality content & production on the platform. To date, the Foundation has funded projects and artists to produce NFTs. The overall valuation of the metaverse grows through the valuation of all games funded by the Foundation, creating a virtuous circle to enable funding for bigger games.

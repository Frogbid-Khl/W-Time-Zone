PROJECT

The Time Zone is a metaverse for watch enthusiasts to trade and share the love of watches. We will create a group of like-minded enthusiasts that allow people to freely shop their watches, display their watches NFTs and even bid on watches. The second hand market for watches is enormous and we will create a web3 marketplace ecosystem for businesses and watch lovers to enjoy and socialize.

Who are we?

We are a group of watch enthusiasts who want to be able to create a more transparent industry for watches. We want to be able to trade our watches more securely and quickly. We also want to check out more information about watches and talk about it whenever we are free. We also want to show off the collections we have.

Why do we need a Metaverse?

Web2 VS Web3
Web2 example of a watch market place - Chrono24
Data: 497k watches in total listed.
500k users per day.
4000 transactions per month, 130 transaction per day.

Pros

-Can quickly browse through products for quick price checking and shopping

-Already existing with large user base

Cons

-no interaction/engagement

-no theme for shops

-centralized and transaction are not transparent

Web3

Pros

-Interaction & Activities Engagement

-Shopping Experience

-Users interaction

-24/7 exhibitions and shops.

-Decentralize mechanism for marketplace with validators (DAO)

-ecosystem built for watch lovers by watch lovers.

-transparent transaction

Cons

-takes time to build, still early stage

-only cryptocurrency payment

-brand new business model

Inside the Time Zone GameFi model, there will be Daily Missions; PVE (Players vs Enemies); PVP (Players vs Players), a rich selection of game modes. Players can play and earn at the same time. They will be rewarded CARROT token, NFT items, Time Zone NFT and more.

Roadmap

Initial Launch (Presale 20th 4PM HKT, Public Launch 21st 4PM HKT)

Time Zone NFT was born in Hong Kong, the first 1,000 pieces were sold out immediately in the 1st round of Presale. After the success in Hong Kong, Time Zone NFT has now expanded its reach to the US, Thailand, and other nations. Many celebrities and collectors have shown keen interests in the Time Zone project. We want to create a strong community filled with fun and games. Our NFT acts as a membership to our community where we will have regular game nights, contests and events with lucrative prizes and raffles. The giveaways will be handed out based on the scoreboard and will be reset monthly. The design of every traits of each Time Zone had gone over many discussions before the finalising. We want to make sure every Time Zone will be unique with some being cute and others being scary. Each Time Zone will be classified by their Scary Meter on deciding which type it will be.

Community

We are a group of people that love games and fun, so we want the community to be like that as well! There will be contests held regularly including but not limited to Trivia, Poker nights, Tabletop games, Puzzle games, Meme contests and many more which some will be decided via voting among the community. There will be scoreboards of each game and the member ranked 1st as well as the most participated member of each month will receive a special 1 of 1 Time Zone.
